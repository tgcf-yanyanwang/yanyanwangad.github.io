Jiaqi Zheng is currently an Assistant Professor from Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University, China. His research area is computer networking, particularly congestion control, programmable data plane, machine learning system and online optimization. He was a Research Assistant at the City University of Hong Kong in 2015 and collaborated with Huawei Noah's Ark Lab. He visited CIS center at Temple University in 2016. He received the Best Paper Award from IEEE ICNP 2015, Outstanding Doctorial Dissertation Award from ACM SIGCOMM China 2018, the First Prize of Jiangsu Science and Technology Award in 2018, Outstanding Doctorial Dissertation Award from CCF, Jiangsu Province and Nanjing University in 2019. He is a member of ACM and IEEE.
